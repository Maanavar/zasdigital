Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our mobile app development process

Native apps are built specifically for iOS (Swift) or Android (Kotlin), offering optimal performance and access to all device features. Cross-platform apps use frameworks like React Native or Flutter to share code between platforms, reducing development time and cost while maintaining good performance. We help you choose the right approach based on your specific requirements, timeline, and budget.

Development time varies based on complexity, features, and platform selection. Simple apps typically take 2-3 months, medium complexity apps 3-6 months, and enterprise-level solutions 6+ months. During discovery, we provide a detailed timeline based on your specific requirements. We use agile methodology with 2-week sprints, providing regular updates and opportunities for feedback.

Our maintenance packages include regular updates for operating system compatibility, bug fixes, performance optimization, security patches, 24/7 monitoring, analytics reporting, and technical support. We offer tiered packages from basic maintenance to full-service support including feature updates and enhancements. All packages include app store compliance updates and emergency support.

We handle the entire app store submission process including preparing all required assets (screenshots, descriptions, icons), configuring app store listings, managing developer accounts, ensuring compliance with guidelines, and submitting to both Apple App Store and Google Play Store. We've developed relationships with app review teams and maintain a 95% first-time approval rate. We also provide post-launch ASO (App Store Optimization) services.
