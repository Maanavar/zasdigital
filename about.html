Our Story

2018 Founded in San Francisco

ZAS Digital began as a small team of passionate technologists with a shared vision: to bridge the gap between innovative ideas and exceptional digital execution. Our founders, Alex Chen and Maria Rodriguez, recognized that while many agencies could build software, few could deliver truly transformative digital experiences.

What started as a boutique studio has grown into a full-service digital agency, but our core philosophy remains unchanged. We believe that technology should serve business objectives, enhance user experiences, and create measurable value.

"Great digital products aren't just built—they're carefully crafted through deep understanding, technical excellence, and relentless attention to detail."

— Alex Chen, Co-Founder & CTO
