Our Story

2020 Founded with a Vision

ZAS Digital was founded in 2020 with a clear mission: to bridge the gap between innovative ideas and exceptional digital execution. Born from the entrepreneurial spirit of our founder, a Computer Science graduate with experience at leading tech companies, we recognized that while many agencies could build software, few could deliver truly transformative digital experiences.

What started as a vision to create meaningful digital solutions has grown into a full-service agency serving global clients. Our journey began with developing educational technology platforms, teaching us valuable lessons about user-centered design and scalable architecture that now benefit all our clients.

"We believe technology should solve real problems, create measurable value, and deliver exceptional experiences that users love."

Senthilkumar Sivaraman, Founder & CEO

Our Journey

Milestones in our growth and evolution

2020

Foundation

ZAS Digital founded with focus on ed-tech solutions. Developed Maanavar platform and began serving our first long-term client, Eflex Energy Systems.

2021

Expansion

Expanded into healthcare technology, working with Texas Children's Hospital. Developed data analytics platforms and news aggregation solutions.

2022

Diversification

Entered e-commerce sector with The Chennai Silks project. Built influencer marketing platforms and expanded our technical stack capabilities.

2023

Specialization

Developed harbor vessel management systems and deepened expertise in logistics technology. Team expanded with domain specialists.

2024

Innovation

Launched restaurant POS systems, recall management platforms, and co-founded EduHire teacher recruitment platform. Focused on AI/ML integration.
